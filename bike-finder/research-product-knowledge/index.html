What big questions will a buyer will be faced with?

• What are the types of bikes - Mountain Bike, Hybrid Bike, Road bike, Electirc bike

• What are the variations and components - Materials, colors, wheels, gear, weight, size, etc

• What is the cost, how can I balance with my budget? - compare to other website's same products or similar prodcuts, what are the "must" to consider and what are not necessary, within some range of budget how can I find the most valuable bike.

• Who is the targeting audience/buyer? - Age range, location, practical, casual

• What is the purpose of purchasing this particular bike?

What priorities might they have?

The top sale brands(reputation), how specific the information is listed, reviews, and which bike is the most suitable to the buyer's purpose of buying, value&price, promotions/deals, (+ is this website providing the best sale?) If not sure about the choices, compare function is effective to have.
